    Ongole: The Epitome of Black Galaxy Exports

    Ongole, a town nestled in the southern part of India, has earned a global reputation as the primary source of high quality Black Galaxy Granite. The region's geology and climate create the ideal conditions for the formation of this exquisite granite variety. Ongole has evolved into a significant hub for granite mining and processing, with numerous quarries and processing units dedicated to extracting, polishing, and exporting this luxurious stone to destinations worldwide.

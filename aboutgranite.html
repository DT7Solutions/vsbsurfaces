    GRANITE

    A granite countertop or flooring brings the beauty of nature into your home, while also providing you with an incredibly tough and wear-resistant surface. Your granite slabs are mined directly from the earth, and since no two of them are the same, it provides your space with a unique appeal. Here’s an overview of the process of making granite slabs.

    1. Procurement

    The first step of making a granite slab is to mine the raw granite materials out of the earth. Using powerful machines, a mining company mines, and blasts raw granite out of the quarry. The first form of granite after mining is a very rough form.

    2. Slab Cutting

    Once the mining process is complete, it is sent to workshops to be transformed into slabs. The stone is then measured and cut into slabs according to the requirements.

    3. Slab Polishing

    With cutting machine we cut the granite and with polishing machine we polish the slabs

    4. Packing

    After you select a slab of your choice, according to the color variations and patterns, we create your custom countertops or floorings according to the measurements and shapes required, which are then packed and installed in your location.

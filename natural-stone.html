    Granite

    VSB Natural Stone Granite is an igneous rock mainly consisting of quartz, feldspar, and mica, which is formed by the solidification of magma under conditions of extreme heat and pressure, making it one of the hardest stones in the world. Its near diamond-hard surface resists heat, scratches, and stains, and protects against mold and mildew. Granite offers an endless variety of different patterns of veins, specks, and swirls which is an ideal material for kitchen and bathroom countertops. From our least expensive to our most expensive stone, every piece is chosen with care!

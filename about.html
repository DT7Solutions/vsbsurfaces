    What We Do?

    VSB Group is an Indian company and one of the largest exporters of Natural and Engineered Stone from India. Speaking about Natural Stone, our team is specialized in procuring rough blocks, processing slabs, and catering to the cut-to-size requirements of our clients in various thicknesses and sizes.

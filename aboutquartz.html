    Quartz

    Prepare for the majestic beauty brought to your spaces by our collection of Engineered Quartz. This is a highly dense product and very close to natural granite. Engineered Quartz is resistant to stains, scratches, and cracks, it is impervious to heat and cold. This supreme material requires minimal maintenance.

    1. Procurement

    The raw materials required for slab production from over 400+ suppliers across the world. Want to be our preferred partners please email to us at operations@vsbquartz.com

    2. Raw Material Screening & Testing

    All the raw materials are tested in our laboratory and grounded as per product parameters.

    3. Batching & Mixing

    The raw materials required for production are loaded into temporary storage units and are grinded to the mixing platform through an automatic convert system. Our process is completely computerized hence accurate.

    4. Pressing and Heating

    The material mixture is spread by a distribution system and is subjected to a pressure of about 9000 psi to form a solid cake. This cake is then heated and subsequently cooled. This facilitates the binding of powdered quartz with organic resins to form a solid surface.

    5. Calibration And Polishing

    The solid cakes are subjected calibration on both sides, trimmed to perfection and polished on our Breton polishing line.

    6. Quality Inspection

    After the slabs are polished they are inspected thoroughly by our quality experts team and grounded accordingly.

    7. Packing and Printing

    Each and every slab is wrapped with polyethene protection to safeguard it from scratches while handling custom labelling and color names are printed at the back of the slabs before they are packed in wooded crates for exports.
